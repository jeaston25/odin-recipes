Description

Here lies a delcious lasagne ready to devour, looks so tasty!

Ingredients

  • Olive Oil
  • Ground Beef
  • Italian Sausage
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Cheeses
  • Lasagne Sheets

Steps

  1. Cook mince and sausage, breaking up chunks and season
  2. Add the sauce and simmer
  3. Stack the lasagne in a tray
  4. Bang it in the oven
  5. Eat, yum!
